How It Works

To find a medical facility near you, first sign in or sign up! Doing this will eliminate the pesky paperwork when you arrive at a doctor’s office. Next, go to the left sidebar to tell us what you need and where you are located. A map will pop up with appropriate facilities for your specific needs. Select the office you prefer to then create and confirm your appointment.

About

Our website is designed to make visiting the doctor a seamless process for both the patient and the doctor. When you create an account with us, you have the ability to send your medical history to any new doctor that you visit, with no paperwork, no fees, and no hassle. Exciting right? We’ve partnered with thousands of medical facilities around the country to ensure that whatever you need and wherever you are, we have you covered. Our easy-to-use website makes getting an appointment a breeze.
